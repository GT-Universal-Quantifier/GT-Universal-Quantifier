## How to Contribute to GT Universal Quantifier

# How can I contribute?
**In every way possible actually. We appreciate every kind of help like:**
- Flowcharts
- Model & Texture Development
- Ideas
- Directly developing the modpack
- Debugging
- Translations
- Suggesting and more, basically everything!

# Expectations from our contributors
- **When contributing, always ask yourself:**

> *"Does this improves accuracy of realism?"*

> *"Does this align with historical or physical logic?"*

> *"Does this respects GT:UQ's philosophy / purposes?"*
